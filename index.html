HealthTranslator

For a better health search on the Web.

View project on GitHub

What is it?

HealthTranslator is a Chrome extension that helps you during your health related searches on the Web. It recognizes medical concepts in Web documents and provides you more information such as definitions, related concepts and external references for a deeper search.

Innovative Features

HealthTranslator differs from the competitors by providing more and faster information. The medical concepts are recognized from vocabularies with thousands of records.

The community can also help by rating the results and suggesting new medical concepts to recognize. That data can be later used for tool improvements.

Supported Languages

It currently supports English and Portuguese.

As of our knowledge, it's the first tool providing Portuguese support.

Highly customizable

The extension's settings are very customizable. You just want it to recognize diseases? Sure, no problem! You can filter the recognized semantic types.

Or maybe just translate Portuguese Web pages? Three clicks. Done.

There are many more options that you may want to explore to fullfil your taste. We want you happy and productive.
